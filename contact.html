Frequently Asked Questions

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day return policy for all items in original condition. Custom pieces are not eligible for returns.

Do you offer international shipping?

Yes, we ship worldwide. International shipping rates and delivery times vary by location.

How do I care for my jewelry?

Store jewelry in a cool, dry place. Clean with a soft cloth and avoid contact with chemicals and water.

Do you offer custom jewelry design?

Yes, we create custom pieces. Contact us with your vision and we'll work with you to bring it to life.
